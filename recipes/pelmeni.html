Pelmeni

Description

Pelmeni are dumplings of Russian cuisine, that consist of a filling wrapped in thin, unleavened dough.

The dough is made from flour and water, while the filling traditionally consists of minced meat, but it can also be made using pork, lamb, beef, or any other kind of meat,
and is usually served alongside sour cream.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Filling of your choice
    •

    Steps

    1. Create the dough by mixing flour and water
    2. Roll the dough out so it is flat
    3. Split the dough into smaller pieces
    4. Fill the split pieces with the meat of your choice
    5. Fill a large pot with water, add some salt, and boil the water
    6. Once the water is boiling, carefully add the pelmeni one by one
    7. Stir the pelmeni from time to time to avoid them sticking together
    8. Once the pelmeni are floating, wait 10-30 seconds, and take them out
    9. The pelmeni is ready to serve, preferably with aditional seasoning and sour cream
