Chili

Description

Chili, otherwise known as Chili con carne, is a spicy stew containing chili peppers, meat, tomatoes and oftenpinto beans or kidney beans.

There are a few ingrediants that are needed, but it is quite easy to make, and not that much time consuming.

Ingredients

  • Large pot
  • Olive
  • Chopped onion
  • Ground beef
  • Chili powder
  • Cumin
  • Sugar
  • Tomato paste
  • Garlic powder
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Heat some olive in the pot
  2. Chop onions, add them, and cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Add ground beef and break it apart
  4. Cook for 6-7 minutes
  5. Add the chili powder, cumin, sugar, tomato paste, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  6. Stir until well combined
  7. Add diced tomatoes, kidney beans, beef broth, and tomato sauce
  8. Stir well
  9. Simmer for 20-25 minutes
  10. Let the chili rest for 5-10 minutes before serving
